# Campaign Performance

## Objective

Evaluate email performance based on engagement quality and intent signals, not just volume metrics.

---

## Overall Performance

- Emails Sent: 77,530  
- Open Rate: 25.78%  
- Total Clicks: 11,042  

### Interpretation

- Open rate indicates strong subject line performance and audience relevance  
- Click volume shows consistent engagement across campaigns  

---

## Intent-Based Performance

- CTA Clicks: 3,751  
- Product Page Visits: 3,013  
- Contact Form Clicks: 412  

### Intent Breakdown

- ~34% of total clicks resulted in CTA engagement  
- ~27% progressed to product-level interest  
- ~4% reached high-conversion intent (contact stage)  

👉 This shows a clear drop-off from engagement → conversion, which is expected in B2B funnels.

---

## Funnel Progression

Email → CTA Click → Product Page → Contact Form

This structure enabled tracking of user movement across the funnel and identifying where optimization was needed.

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Engagement Quality > Volume
High click volume alone is not enough.  
Tracking downstream actions revealed real buying intent.

---

### 2. Mid-Funnel Strength
Strong performance at the CTA and product engagement level indicates effective content and messaging.

---

### 3. Conversion Opportunity
Lower contact-form interaction highlights opportunity to improve:

- CTA clarity  
- Landing page experience  
- Conversion messaging  

---

## Optimization Actions

- Increased use of product-focused content for high-engagement users  
- Improved CTA positioning and clarity  
- Aligned content with user intent stages  

---

## Business Impact

- Enabled visibility into real user behavior  
- Improved decision-making based on intent signals  
- Strengthened email as a demand generation channel  

---

## Key Insight

Performance improves when metrics are analyzed across the full funnel, not just at the top level.
