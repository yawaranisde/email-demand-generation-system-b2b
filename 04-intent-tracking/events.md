# Intent Tracking System

## Why This Matters

Opens and clicks do not reflect real buying intent.  
This system tracks deeper engagement signals.

---

## Events Tracked

| Event | Meaning | Funnel Stage |
|------|--------|-------------|
| CTA Click | User shows active interest | Mid-funnel |
| Product Page Visit | User evaluates offering | Consideration |
| Contact Form Click | User ready to convert | Bottom-funnel |

---

## How It Was Used

- Identified high-intent users  
- Improved lead prioritization  
- Optimized campaign performance  

---

## Key Insight

Not all clicks are equal. Intent tracking improves conversion quality.
