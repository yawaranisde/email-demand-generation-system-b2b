# Intent Tracking System

## Objective

Measure real user intent beyond basic metrics (opens and clicks) to identify high-value prospects and improve conversion outcomes.

---

## Why This Matters

Traditional email metrics (open rate, CTR) do not indicate buying readiness.

A user clicking a link is not the same as a user exploring a product or initiating contact.

This system focuses on **behavioral signals that reflect actual intent**.

---

## Event Framework (Intent Hierarchy)

| Event | Meaning | Intent Level | Funnel Stage |
|------|--------|-------------|-------------|
| CTA Click | Initial interest in content | Medium | Mid-funnel |
| Product Page Visit | Active evaluation of solution | High | Consideration |
| Contact Form Click | Strong intent to engage | Very High | Bottom-funnel |

---

## How Intent Was Interpreted

- Multiple CTA clicks → repeated interest  
- Product page visits → solution awareness and evaluation  
- Contact interactions → readiness for sales engagement  

Users were grouped based on **depth of interaction**, not just activity.

---

## How It Was Used

### 1. Lead Prioritization
- High-intent users (form clicks, product visits) prioritized for sales follow-up  

### 2. Campaign Optimization
- Content adjusted based on which events were increasing  
- High-performing content types scaled  

### 3. Funnel Analysis
- Identified drop-off points between engagement → conversion  
- Improved transition between email tiers  

---

## Example Flow

Email → CTA Click → Product Page → Contact Form  

Each step increases confidence that the user is moving toward conversion.

---

## Business Impact

- Improved lead quality entering the pipeline  
- Reduced wasted effort on low-intent users  
- Enabled data-driven decision-making  

---

## Key Insight

Not all engagement signals are equal.  
Tracking intent depth provides a clearer view of buyer readiness and improves conversion efficiency.
