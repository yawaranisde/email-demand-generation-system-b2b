# Audience Strategy

## Objective

Build a high-quality, high-intent email audience that can be converted into qualified leads.

---

## Problem

The existing email audience was unstructured:

- Lists were sourced from events with low relevance  
- No segmentation based on intent or role  
- High number of inactive and unengaged contacts  

Result: Low engagement and poor conversion potential.

---

## Approach

### 1. Audience Cleanup

- Removed inactive and unengaged contacts  
- Filtered outdated event-based data (NAPM, NPF)  
- Improved overall list quality and deliverability  

---

### 2. Audience Rebuild

- Acquired new contacts through targeted campaigns  
- Focused on industries with high relevance to Assurety’s solutions  

---

### 3. Segmentation Logic

Audience was structured based on relevance and intent:

- Industry-based segmentation  
- Role-based targeting (decision-makers)  
- Engagement-based filtering (active vs inactive users)  

---

## Target Segments

- Commercial mailers  
- Financial institutions  
- USPS partner companies  

---

## Why This Matters

Email performance depends more on **who receives the message** than how many emails are sent.

A smaller, high-intent audience delivers:

- Higher engagement rates  
- Better conversion potential  
- More efficient campaigns  

---

## Key Insight

Audience quality drives performance.  
Cleaning and structuring the audience improved engagement and enabled more effective campaign targeting.
