# Content Architecture

## 3-Tier Email System

### Tier 1 — Thought Leadership
- Purpose: Awareness  
- Content: AI insights and industry knowledge  

### Tier 2 — Testimonials
- Purpose: Trust  
- Content: Client success stories  

### Tier 3 — Product Education
- Purpose: Conversion  
- Content: Product features and use cases  

## Key Insight

Content aligned with buyer stage increases engagement and conversions.
